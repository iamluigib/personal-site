Professionalism & Quality

Those are the two ideas that the client wanted to convey with her company's brand. Alyssa Workman wanted her independently owned hair and nail salon to be attractive, approachable, and scream professionalism. She was looking to create an image for her brand that represented those qualities that she could use on all of her marketing and promotional material, including her online presence.

The Look and Feel

The owner wanted a color palette and design that was clean, professional, and attractive but still represented herself. Her regular clients are mostly younger to middle aged women. Her physical salon is adorned with mandalas, so we decided to make that the defining image of her logo. The mandala is to represent the creative and expressive personality of the owner. To complement the mandala, we have her brand name draped over the top of the mandala. The font is in Montserrat, a clean and modern looking font, with the curve taking away some of the cold-ness of the font. The words underneath that briefly describe the business are straight, keeping the coldness to express professionalism. When displaying the logo, we determiend that the heavy use of imagery was the best method to making a eye-catching and attractive layout. For the remainder of the brand, we decided to take the minimalist approach. A dark gray is used as the base color, representing stability, curiosity, and professionalism without the negativity attached to black. Sparing splashes of purple are used to catch the eye and avoid a soulless look on non-physical medium. For physical medium, you can see heavy use of metallic silver, a design lifted from the salon interior.
