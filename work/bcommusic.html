In a world where the music industry is dominated by computer algorithms, Bcom Music is looking to put the humanity back in music discovery. Started by a group of friends, their plan is to have curators with unique tastes in music do the hard work of finding fantastic music so you don't have to.

Putting discovery on center stage

A website-only service, Bcom Music's focus was placed on discovery. Discovering artists who deserve some of the spotlight, discovering songs curated by people, and discovering playlists for a hands-off music expereince.
